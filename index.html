How to Use SpeedTrackify

Get instant speed readings anywhere, anytime — no app downloads required!

Install for Offline Use

Access SpeedTrackify even without internet:

  • Open the menu (3 dots) in your browser
  • Select "Add to Home Screen" or "Install"
  • Access SpeedTrackify directly from your home screen

Troubleshooting Tips

For best performance:

  • Ensure your device's GPS is enabled
  • Grant location permissions when prompted
  • Use in open areas for better GPS accuracy
  • Try a different browser if you experience issues
